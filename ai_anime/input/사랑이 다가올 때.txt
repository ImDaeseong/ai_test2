Title: 사랑이 다가올 때
Genre: This hyper-Kpop fusion bursts with high-tempo industrial pop, opening with metallic synth stabs and distorted bass layered over Daeguem glitch motifs and rapid Kkwaenggwari percussion. The verses feature sharp falsetto and stutter-gated, granular vocal chops, weaving a futuristic cyberpunk texture anchored in Korean traditional scales. Sudden binaural rewind FX create spatial twists, while glitch elements and relentless beats keep energy peaking, delivering an experimental, high-impact sound.
Weirdness: 25%
Style Influence: 85%
